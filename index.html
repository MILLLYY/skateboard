MY STORY

The Beginning

I always envied the skater kids,i longed for skater friends and the freedom that skateboarding promised.I was always fascinated by the culture that surrounded it. Even as a kid,i would imagine myself competing in the olympics.Before i could afford a board,i kept my dream alive by watching YouTube videos and skateboarding movies, fueling my passion from afar.My journey truly began when i met the very first boy i saw that skated in my neighborhood at the local park. He rolled in on a longboard and without hesitation,i asked him to teach me how to skate.Lucky for me he was kind enough and soon enoug, i found myself joining him and his friends for spontaneous skate sessions,discovering that my hood was filled with hidden skatimg talent.

The Journey

It was not an easy road really ,there were countless falls,bruises and failures,mostly failures that eventually taught me resilence.I started with a low quility board that i got from a local chinese shop,which was the worst mistake ever.the board was barely good enough for learning, but every tumble pushed me to keep going. When i upgraded to a curver ,everything began to shift.I fell in love with the new board as i mastered curving and pumping,each ride reinforcing that the thrill was in the learning itself.

Without friends around to share the vibe,as i lost touch with my first skater friend and his friends.I decided to bring someone on my journey.I bought cruiser for my little brother so we could skate together.He became my unexpected skate buddy and teaching him only deepened my own skills.We shared laughs,pains,falls and countless moments of the triumph,making every session on the board even more special.Till today i have not been to any skate session with people my age because everyone dissapeared,my hood is a ghost town of skaters.Yet,that experience laid a foundation of my love for all forms of skating.

Today whenever i feel the urge to ride ,i simply grab whichever board that matches my mood .Whether it's a skateboard,a curver or my little brother's criuser.I still dream of adding a londboard to my collection,a tributed to the first friend who sparked my passion and a way to cruising the streets in a whole new style.I enjoy all types of skating and skateboards,hence i have never really focused on mastering one style or skateboard.I am all about the fun and the freedom of the ride,being pro was never really my goal because i skate simply for the love of it and the challenge of learning new tricks everyday.I am not really just a skateboarder any more,i am an emature skater who is driven by the curiosity and the joy of exploring every new tecniquue and personality a board can bring out in me and thrill of constant learning,the joy of self expression and the freedom that comes with every ride.

THE FLIP SIDE

Essentail Tricks

  • Ollie
    The most basic trick in skateboarding,this is the foundation jump that sets up most other tricks.
  • Kickflip and Heelflip
    These are the first flip tricks that you will learn,they involve flipping the board using your feet.
  • Pop Shuvit
    This is a very fun trick that involves spinning the board in a 180 degrees rotation without flipping it.
  • Manuals,Grinds,Wheelies,Slides and many more
    Once you have mastered the basics,you can move on to more advanced tricks like these to enhance your balance and control while riding.

Skateboarding Tactics

  • Start with the basic and practice consistently.
  • Always wear protective gear such as helmets and pads for safety.
  • Warm up properly and focus on proper form to prevent injuries.
  • Visit skate parks ,make friends and learn from experienced skaters.
  • Enjoy the journey and celebrate every small victory.
  • Don't be afraid to fall,each fall is a stepping stone to improvement.
  • Above all, have fun.

GEAR UP

For me, every piece of gear is not just about functionality,it also reflects my personal style and passion ,but most of all i am big on comfortability.I take pride in customising and maintaining my equipment,ensuring i am always safe and confident to cruise around the streets and hit the skate park,to learn new tricks and push my practice sessions to the next level.So i am just gonna walk you through a few important gear essentials that you need to get you started:

  • Skateboard

    It consists of the following parts:

    1. Deck
    2. Trucks
    3. Wheels
    4. Bearings
    5. Grip tape

    Try and make sure that you get your self a good/high quality skateboard that will last you a long time and that balances performance with durability.You might think that it doesn't matter but trust me quality is very important when choosing a board because it will affect your performance and overall experience.

  • Protective Gear

    These are safety items which consists of the:

    1. helmet
    2. wrist guards
    3. knee pads
    4. elbow pads

    Safety is top priority,so make sure you wear these every time you skate especially when your just starting out.These will keep you safe during falls, so you can practice confidently and push your limits without worry.

  • Skate Shoes

    Make sure you try and get skater shoes or shoes with good grip,this will help you from slipping off your board.They offer excellent grip and comfort,ensuring you maintain full control over your board even during intense maneuvers.
